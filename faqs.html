Faq's

Frequently Asked Questions

Can we meet you before we book?

We will be more than glad to meet you before you book our events. You can tell us all about the way you want your wedding to be crafted, so we can make sure every part of the event that we plan resonates with your dream.

You can also meet for an informal and no obligation meet-up about your wedding plan. If you are a bit further, I would be more than happy to make a video call and talk things through.

Can we look at the venue before booking?

Of course. With our venue selection service, we will be more than glad to go with you and show you some of the best selection of venues that resonates with your wedding theme, times, and budget.

What does full-service wedding planning include?

Our full-service Wedding planning includes everything from choosing the venue for your event, decoration, entertainment, bridal makeover, photography, videography, and much more. All you have to do is contact us with your specific needs and relax with the assurance that everything will be taken care of by us.

How will our wedding pictures be delivered?

Your wedding pictures will be delivered all edited, in a digital format. You can also order a printed album of a specific number of photos per requirement.

Can we add on hours if our wedding runs a little long?

If there is any change on your timeline plan, just give us a heads up and relax. We will add hours to the plan while ensuring that you get the most from our wedding planning service.

Do you help in the planning process?

With experience with a massive number of weddings up close, we can help you with your planning process and make your big day as hassle-free as possible.

Our goal is to take the matter into our hands, so all there is left for you to do is enjoy the big day without a shred of worry in your mind.
