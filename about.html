About Us

The Wedding Library

Hello there!

Simple words will not be able to describe how exceptional the wedding library is. But I will try to describe nonetheless. From the moment you meet us, you will instantly know that your wedding is in safe hands, and that is a promise.

We like amazing people like you, capturing their true connections, and making your event into a unique and fully customized experience. We are an all-in-one wedding planning and décor solution within budget.

We offer our assistance to seek out your truest form of emotions and share your amazing story through your dream decor. If this means something important to you and your better half, then you have found the best wedding planner for your dream event.

Phaneesh Kumar Polisetty (The founder)

Being a full-time wedding planner is not just a full-time job for me. It is something that I hold dear to my heart and am genuinely passionate about. I and my team are always looking to go above and beyond to make sure your wedding event not only looks beautiful but will hold a deeper meaning to my amazing couples.

With this strong passion for executing flawless events, I started The Wedding Library, an all one wedding decorator service in Bangalore. My goal is to break down every part of your wedding planning, pay attention to the smallest of details, and transform it into a memory you will never forget.

Phaneesh
